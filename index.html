About
01

Art That Swings Different

⭐ Currently Serving — United States Army

Triple Double Bats is the creative vision of Dewayne Walker — a proud U.S. Army soldier and self-taught artist who transforms standard baseball bats into handcrafted works of art. Every bat tells a story: team pride, fraternal identity, personal legacy.

From bold fraternity designs to custom portrait pieces and sports team tributes, no two bats are alike. Each creation is painted by hand with meticulous detail, built to be displayed, gifted, or carried with pride.
